Bachelor of Science in Computer Science

Overview:

The computer science major is designed to prepare students for graduate study in advanced computer science as well as entry level positions in the computer industry. The computer science department offers a selection of courses covering programming and software development, design and analysis of computer systems, computer architecture, computer theory, and computer appoptioncations.
